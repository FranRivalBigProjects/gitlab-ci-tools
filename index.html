Frequently Asked Questions

Why is my GitLab CI pipeline slow?

Poor job design, lack of caching, or inefficient runners.

What is a GitLab CI pipeline?

A sequence of automated jobs defined in a YAML configuration.

How can I optimize CI?

Use caching, parallel jobs, and efficient runner configuration.

What is a job graph?

A visualization of job dependencies and execution order.

How do I debug failures?

Check logs, validate YAML, and isolate failing steps.
